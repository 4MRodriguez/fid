Work I've Done for Casselberry Elementary

I've been volunteering at Casselberry for years, and I love being able to help provide fun events that the kids can enjoy and learn from. Some of my favorites have been "Art Night," where I ran a watercolor station, and "Science Night," where I ran a station teaching about the liver (using real cow liver!) I've helped come up with station ideas for art night, and even painted a giant collaborative piece for this year. Additionally, I've also helped in the library by making Disney themed decorations.
