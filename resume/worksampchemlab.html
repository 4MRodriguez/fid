Work I've Done for Clubs

Chem Lab Crew:

I joined Chem Lab Crew last year and immediately loved it. My friends and I came up with an idea to make popping boba as a fundraiser, so we organized everything: doing the math for measurements and cost, the materials needed, and working out the perfect recipe. It went extremely well, and the Vice Principal has asked that we make it a yearly thing! We were able to make more money than we had previously, so we're excited to do more chemistry experiments and fundraisers this year! I also put some images of other things: the Chem Lab Crew T-shirt I designed, bismuth crystals we made, gold plated pennies, and borax crystals and carbonated fruit, both of which we gave to all the staff for teacher appreciation week.
